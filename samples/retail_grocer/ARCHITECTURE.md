## Key Measures Across Domains

### Sales Domain
- TotalSales, LFLSales, SalesGrowthYoY
- AvgBasketValue, GrossMarginPct
- OnlineSalesMix

### Inventory Domain  
- InStockRate (OSA), DaysOfSupply
- StockTurnover, GMROI
- OTIF (supplier performance)

### Customer Domain
- TotalCustomers, CustomerRetentionRate
- AvgCLV, ShopFrequency
- LoyaltyPenetration

### Promotions Domain
- PromoSalesMix, PromoROI
- PromoLift, AvgDiscountDepth

### Operations Domain
- ConversionRate, SalesPerSqFt
- WasteRate, LabourCostPct
- SalesPerLabourHour
